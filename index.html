DO NOT LET HIM DIE, FOR THE APPARATUS DEPENDS ON HIM!

For the sake of the Apparatus, you must manage the creature's:

- ENERGY
- LEVELS
- CLONING PROCESSES

All areas must remain at the highest setting. Click to replenish if an area gets low.
If all fields reach the lowest setting, he will die.

If a cloning attempt fails, start a new cloning process.
BEWARE: Beginning a new cloning process will drain his energy.

View Him, or view the Apparatus. Both He and the Apparatus will show signs of breakdown before total failure.
Once total failure occurs, it cannot be solved except by the 'RESET' button.
